ABOUT US

Abhivyakti, the annual cultural festival of Shri Ramswaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management provides a platform for students to showcase their unique talents and are important in the holistic development of the personality of the students, providing a break from the monotonous routine of evaluation and academics to the students. The three day extravaganza attracts participants not only from various colleges of Uttar Pradesh but also from other states as well. The unique feature of this fest is its management involving the participation of each and every student of the college in some way or the other. Every year various cultural events and competitions are organized which include music, singing, dance, fashion, drama etc. The cultural extravaganza which showcases an envious talent pool of various hobby clubs of the college culminates with a massive rock fest Xero and thereafter with a Bollywood Starnight. While preserving the spirit and zest of our Cultural Festival, Abhivyakti is scheduled from April 28 to April 30, 2022 to keep the enthusiasm high and the legacy ever golden.
